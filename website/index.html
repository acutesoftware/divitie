Divitie

The latin phrase "divitiae divitie" means riches, wealth. Divitie is a game where you build wealth - how you define wealth is up to you. you can have wealth by do it through trading game, where you collect, build and trade goods on a beautiful 3D world. You can be a nice person and help others. You can study / learn and be the best at your craft. Or you can rise to a position of power.

What is Divitie?

Divitie means wealth or riches - but this is not limited to money or assets. This game lets you choose how you want to progress to the end game - you can spend your time working, making a business, crafting, or living a happy and relaxed life.

Wealth is up to you - what you value.

Stategies for play

You will not be able to max out all areas of wealth as there is simply not enough time in the day. You have to decide which areas to focus on.

It is a good idea to have a 'little' wealth in all areas, and you can choose either
  • a) focus on 1 area and get to the top fast
  • b) focus on a couple of areas
  • c) live a well balanced life [there is a separate achievement for this, not counting the standard measures of wealth]

    How the game works

    You have 50 years to achieve wealth.
    You set your character on the path you want by choosing those quests.
    Offline mode allows characters to train / practice when you are not online
